Plugin

  • Client : CREALP
  • Date : 01/2017
  • Used Technologies : C#, WPF

Within the framework of the CRUEX++ project, both a methodology and a computational tool for estimation of extreme floods have been developed for dam safety purpose. The developed methodology allows to estimate the hydrograph of the safety flood based on the PMP-PMF approach (probable maximum precipitation-probable maximum flood). The obtained peak discharge is compared to the results of statistical extrapolation.
